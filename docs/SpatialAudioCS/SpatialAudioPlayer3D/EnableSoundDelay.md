# SpatialAudioPlayer3D.EnableSoundDelay property

## When enabled, playback is delayed based on the distance between the emitter and the listener divided by [param speed_of_sound], simulating the finite travel time of sound through air.

Only affects the initial `Play()` call. Once playback has started it runs at normal speed. Best suited for one-shot sounds (gunshots, explosions, impacts).
